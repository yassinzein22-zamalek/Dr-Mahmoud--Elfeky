Meet Dr. Elfeky

Where clinical knowledge meets innovation.

Dr. Mahmoud Elfeky is an orthodontist, TMD specialist and AI consultant with over 12 years of experience in healthcare and innovation. He holds a Master’s degree in Orthodontics and a Diploma in Laser Applications in Medicine from Cairo University.

He is a member of the Egyptian Orthodontic Society and serves as Medical Manager and Speaker for Forestadent Germany. His work connects patient care, education and practical healthcare technology.
